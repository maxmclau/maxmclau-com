About

In the past 18 months talk of UFO sightings passed from tin-hat support groups to the hallowed pages of the New York Times and Washington Post. The pentagon is going on record, and we’ve got declassified military footage. What’s happening? Why does it seem like there is a new UFO story every week lately? Supercluster’s own Robin Seemangal is joined by Daniel Oberhaus, WIRED space reporter and author of the upcoming book Extraterrestrial Languages, to sort it all out.

Together they are among the few space journalists that have attempted to critically investigate UFO phenomenon over the years, crossing paths with concerned Apollo astronauts, the Clinton '16 campaign, and eventually, each other.
